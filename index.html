The Problem: A Two-Sided Crisis

1. Clinician Burnout

Doctors, nurses, and clinicians are overwhelmed. They spend an average of 2-3 hours *per day* on administrative tasks and data entry, leading to widespread fatigue and taking time away from direct patient care.

2. Diagnostic Errors

Critical patient information is often buried within thousands of words of unstructured, free-text clinical notes. Key symptoms and historical data are easily missed, contributing to diagnostic errors that affect 1 in 20 adults.

The Solution: An Intelligent Co-pilot

  • What it is: A GenAI platform that securely integrates with existing EHR systems.
  • Output 1: Factual Summary: It transforms chaotic notes into a concise, scannable summary of the patient's condition, history, and status.
  • Output 2: Prioritized Diagnoses: It generates a list of potential differential diagnoses based on the note's content.
  • The Innovation: This isn't just a summary. The *justified diagnosis* with direct text references is our key innovation, acting as a true diagnostic aid.

Feature 1: Factual Summarization

From Chaos to Clarity

Our first key feature extracts all relevant patient history, symptoms, medications, and lab results, presenting them in a scannable, standardized format. This allows clinicians to understand a complex case in seconds, not 30 minutes.

Feature 2: Hypothesis Generation

  • Beyond Summary: We move from simple summarization to actionable clinical insight.
  • Prioritized Diagnoses: The model generates a list of potential differential diagnoses, ranked by probability based on the note's content.
  • Ensured Traceability: This is our key differentiator. The model provides **traceability**, referencing the *exact* part of the note that justifies each hypothesis, eliminating "black box" AI.
  • Reduced Errors: By surfacing possibilities, we help clinicians avoid cognitive biases and reduce the risk of missed diagnoses.

The Impact: A 360° Benefit

For Patients

Better, faster, and more accurate diagnoses. Reduces the risk of "missed" symptoms and improves overall quality of care.

For Clinicians

Saves 2-3 hours per day, drastically reducing administrative burden and burnout. Restores focus to patient interaction.

For Hospitals

Improves diagnostic accuracy, enhances operational efficiency, and reduces costly errors, leading to better system-wide outcomes.

How It Works: The "Secret Sauce"

1. Input Note

Unstructured, free-text clinical notes are securely fed into the CLinIQ AI engine.

2. LLM + RAG

Our model uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to cross-reference the note with a trusted medical knowledge base (like PubMed).

3. Verified Output

This grounding prevents AI "hallucinations" and produces a medically accurate summary and justified diagnoses.
